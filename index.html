Bio

The Heavy are a rock band from England formed in 2007. They are most well known for "How You Like Me Now" which was released in 2009 on their album "The House That Dirt Built".


Discography

  2. The House That Dirt Built (2009)
  3. The Glorious Dead (2012)
  4. Hurt & the Merciless (2016)
  5. Sons (2019)
